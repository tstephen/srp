1. Abatement interventions

This module was developed within the Healthy Returns 2020 project. You can see the results and access the tool here Healthy Returns 2020.

1.1. National carbon cost benefit curve

1.1.1. Actor

Staff of NHS provider organisation

1.1.2. User story

The national carbon and cost benefit curve presents the potential for savings in the NHS, public health and social care sector showing a range of interventions and investments. Savings are shown for the health and care sector in England assuming moderate implementation in five years.

1.1.3. User outcome

1.1.4. Status

1.2. National organisation type carbon and cost benefit curve

1.2.1. Actor

Staff of NHS provider organisation

1.2.2. User story

The national organisation type carbon and cost benefit curve shows the interventions for a specific type of organisation. Savings are shown assuming a moderate implementation in five years for the organisations of this type in England.

1.3. Your carbon and cost benefit curve

1.3.1. Actor

Staff of NHS provider organisation

1.3.2. User story

This interactive tool is to support organisation planning for carbon and cost savings available. Average organisation size is used by default or you can enter your own data for planning. You can also add data for other interventions specific to your organisation to see the information alongside existing interventions.
